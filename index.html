Why Choose Green Lake Campground?

Golfing

The campgrounds Par 3 nine-hole golf course is a tricky 1300 yards long with holes ranging from 57 to 178 yards. The course is great for kids and adults of all ages, from beginners to experts. The course is free with unlimited play for campers so donï¿½t forget to bring your clubs!

Fishing

Bring your poles and catch a big one in our newly expanded fishing hole....Whitetail Springs! Whether you are a beginner, an avid angler, or just teaching a kid to fish for the first time...Whitetail Springs is sure to bring you enjoyment. The best part is no fishing license is required!

Camping

Green Lake Campground has spacious seasonal sites for campers who enjoy all the amenities and activities we have to offer, from April thru October. Our seasonal community is what makes GLC go from good to great!

Aquatics

Relax and Enjoy!
At Green Lake Campground, you are never too far from water. We have water parks with fountains and features for kids and an adult-only pool for grown-ups to relax and enjoy. There is a splash park, family pool, a new sport pool, and much more.
