Seasonal Camping!

Green Lake Campground has spacious seasonal sites for campers who enjoy all the amenities and activities we have to offer, from April thru October. Our seasonal community is what makes GLC go from good to great!

Benefits of seasonal camping include not having to tow a camper, nor spend precious time setting up and taking down camp. Seasonal campers are able to focus on the most important thing the second they drive through our gates... having a good time!

All seasonal sites have full hook-ups and cable television. For information regarding seasonal sites at Green Lake Campground or Whispering Oaks, call us at 920-294-3543.
