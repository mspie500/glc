Top Amenities

Nine-Hole Golf Course

The campground's Par 3 nine-hole golf course is 1300 yards. The course is great for kids and adults of all ages. The course is free with unlimited play for campers!

Stocked Fishing Pond

Whether you are a beginner or an avid angler...Whitetail Springs is sure to bring you enjoyment. No fishing license is required!

Nature Trails

Green Lake Campground has beautiful nature trails that offer terrific views and challenging adventures!

Four Swimming Pools

At Green Lake Campground, we have a splash park, a family pool, a sport pool, and an adult-only pool for grown-ups to relax and enjoy.

Outdoor Amenities

Hay Rides

Take a hay ride throughout the campground and surrounding areas!

Beach & Swimming Pond

Relax on our beautiful beach or take a swim in our crystal clear pond.

Playground

Let the kids have a blast on our state of the art playground.

Outdoor Movies

Watch a classic film under the stars with us!

Athletic Amenities

Sand Volleyball

Get a team together and have some fun on our sand volleyball court.

Basketball

Shoot around by yourself or get a game of three on three going.

Mini Golf

Our wacky mini golf course is a great time for golfers and non-golfers of all ages.

Horseshoes

Test your luck on one of our horseshoe pits.

Live Music
